Ashianna Kim

Ashianna Kim is a child who dreams of becoming a flight attendant. She is an only child and spends more time with her friends than with her parents. She is close to her aunt. She often goes to a bar where she meets different men. One time, at a friend's birthday party, she saw a man she liked, but he disappeared. Later, she meets Hiro.

Akihiro Leonel

Hiro dreams of becoming a pilot. He comes from a wealthy family and studies Mechanical Engineering at DLSU. His family owns Philippine Airlines. He meets Ashianna (Yanna) at a bar and they become friends, eventually falling in love. However, they drift apart because Hiro must leave the country to study. They reunite when Hiro discovers they have a child.

Sam

Sam is Yanna's closest friend, and she's always been by her side. Yanna has grown to dislike him because Sam has been there for Yanna through all her problems. While they have many friends, Sam stands out as being different. He has his own struggles, but despite that, he remains by Yanna's side. When Yanna got pregnant, Sam was the one who stayed with her, but they kept the identity of the father a secret from their other friends. The truth was that Sam was the father, but they didn't want others to know. Avi, Yanna's son, has a wonderful aunt who is always there for him. She's the best aunt anyone could ask for.

Avi

Avi is Yanna's only child, where he grew up without seeing his father, growing up with his aunt Sam. Yanna often left him there because she had a job. Avi also dreams of becoming a flight attendant so he can travel to different places such as Japan. He is a happy and mischievous child but in the end Avi meets his father Hiro who is a successful pilot there and they live a happy and complete family.
